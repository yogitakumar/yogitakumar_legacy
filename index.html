About Me

Hello! I'm Yogita, Software Developer based in London, UK. I enjoy creating Mobile/Web application, including backend services and integration with external systems. My goal is to build application high in performance and user friendly.

I have done my Master’s in Computers and hold 5+ year of professional experience in teaching and IT. I started my career as Software professional in July'2010, and later moved to teaching in Jan’2012, educating under graduate and post graduate students. After working for 4 years, I took time off to enjoy motherhood and later joined start up RIED LABS in Oct'2015.

Moved to UK in July 2017 and took time off for parenting care and second maternity. Though I've been out of the workforce, I have stayed current with industry trends and continued my online learing to keep my skills updated.

I am Founding member of TechPowerGirls , which aims to empower moms with career break to resume their career. With my not for profit community, I have successfully trained 100+ moms in Flutter App Development, delivering workshop/seminars. As a hobby, I write blogs on technology and review software products.

At this point of time, I feel myself confident to resume my full time career in Technology and thankful to TechReturns for providing oppurtunity to reshape my career.
